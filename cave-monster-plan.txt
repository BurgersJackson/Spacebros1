Cave Monster Boss Plan

Spawner
- Replace cave cruiser spawner call with createCaveMonsterBoss() selecting 3 new monster bosses by gateIndex (0/1/2).
- Keep loot drops and arena logic unchanged.

Monsters
- CaveMonster1 uses monster1.png, HP 250, slow ring rotation.
- CaveMonster2 uses monster2.png, HP 300, medium ring rotation.
- CaveMonster3 uses monster4.png, HP 350, fast ring rotation.
- Movement: M1 strafe/hover, M2 aggressive chase, M3 slow artillery.

Shield System (Crystalline Tech Panels)
- Two concentric shield rings with 50 shard slots each.
- Every other slot active: 25 indestructible shards per ring, permanent gaps.
- Rings rotate in opposite directions.
- Same inner/outer radii for all three monsters.
- Bullets collide with shield arcs; hull damage only when gaps align.
- Other ships collide at outer ring radius (no ramming into hull).

Escalation (Shorter Cave Timings)
- Phase 1: 0-40s, normal attacks, 1.0x fire rate.
- Phase 2: 40-80s, 1.3x fire rate, extra attack layers, faster ring spin.
- Phase 3: 80-120s+, 1.5x fire rate, full kit, fastest ring spin.

Collision Hulls (Accurate Bullet Collision)
- Use multi-sphere hullDefinition per monster based on sprite silhouette.
- Implement hitTestCircle() like Destroyer, with hullScale for sprite size.
- Set collisionRadius for ship-ship collisions to outer ring radius.

Attack Kits (3-4 unique each)
Monster 1 (Bio-Mechanical)
- Wing Barrage: alternating pod fire.
- Drone Swarm: small homing bombs.
- Siphon Beam: telegraphed slow/damage.
- Tendril Mines: area denial.

Monster 2 (Berserker)
- Gore Charge: dash with trail.
- Horn Arc: wide crescent spread.
- Bellow Shockwave: push back.
- Blood Rain: localized falling shots.

Monster 3 (Dreadnought)
- Spine Salvo: sequential hardpoint fire.
- Plasma Mortar: lobbed explosive shells.
- Gravity Well: pull zone.
- Shield Drone: orbiting blocker.

Assets
- Load monster1.png, monster2.png, monster4.png as enemy textures.
